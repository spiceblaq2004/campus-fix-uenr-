Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our repair services

How long do repairs take?

Most repairs are completed within 2-4 hours. Screen replacements and battery changes are usually same-day services. Complex issues like water damage may take 1-2 days.

Do you offer warranty?

Yes! Screen repairs come with 6 months warranty, batteries with 1 year warranty, and other repairs with 3-6 months warranty depending on the service.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the entire UENR campus including all hostels - Republic Hall, Independence Hall, Freedom Hall, and all other campus residences.

Do I need to pay before repair?

No payment is required until your phone is repaired and you're satisfied with the service. We believe in earning your trust first.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept mobile money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), cash, and bank transfers. Whatever is most convenient for you!

Can I get my data back after repair?

We always backup your data before starting any repair. In 98% of cases, all your data remains intact. For complex issues, we'll inform you beforehand.
